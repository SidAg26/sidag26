Architecture

A serverless MAPE-inspired loop for RL: Measure rollouts, Analyze rewards, Plan updates, Execute policy changes‚Äîimplemented via OpenFaaS functions.

üß≠

Control Plane

Env Workers

Challenges & Mitigations

  • ‚Ä¢ Cold starts/latency: keep minimal warm pools; bundle deps.
  • ‚Ä¢ Statelessness: externalize replay buffers/checkpoints (S3/DB).
  • ‚Ä¢ Time/memory limits: route heavy updates to GPU nodes/batch jobs.
  • ‚Ä¢ Debugging distributed runs: centralize logs, traces, and metrics.

Getting Started

  3. Configure external storage for trajectories and checkpoints.
  4. Invoke rollout/evaluation functions and monitor via Gateway/Prometheus.
